## How E-commerce AI Imaging Works

These AI models understand physics, lighting, and reflections. When you place a perfume bottle on an AI-generated marble counter, the tool calculates accurate light refractions and cast shadows to make the composition photorealistic.

## The Best AI Product Photography Tools

### 1. Photoroom
Photoroom specializes in instantly removing backgrounds and dropping products into hundreds of high-quality HD, pre-made studio settings.

### 2. Pebblely
Dubbed "magic for products," Pebblely is famous among Shopify owners for taking boring white-background pictures and integrating them seamlessly into complex scenes.

### 3. Flair AI
Flair is a powerful tool tailored specifically for CPG (Consumer Packaged Goods), offering unparalleled drag-and-drop customization over props and lighting.
